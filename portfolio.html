Project 1.1.9 an interactive Sponge-Bob themed Tic Tac Toe.
A program made into a sponge-bob themed tic tac toe.

With the icons you will place theme in the grid with the end goal of getting three in a row.





Project 1.2.5 Wordsmith a typing game with the theme of a skyline.
The objective of the game is to type the words correctly that are presented in the bubble.
Earn a point everytime you spell a word correctly, but if a mistake is made you loose a point.
When a mistake is made a cloud will turn into a red cloud to indicate the mistake.
For every three words correctly spelt three more clouds with words will appear.





Project 1.3.1 Mancala and two player turn-based game.
Each pit in the beginning has four marbles.
The objective of the game is to recieve the most marbles in your pit.
From the picture player one is winning becuase the have two marbles in their pit.
At the beginning of the game it prompts you with four questions.
The questions are the following: what color whould you like the background, what color would you like the board, what name would you like for player one and for player two.
